## Overview

BlueLearnerHub respects the intellectual property rights of others and expects our users to do the same. This DMCA (Digital Millennium Copyright Act) Takedown Policy describes how we handle copyright infringement claims and our obligations under India's Information Technology Act, 2000.

**Note**: While the DMCA is a United States law, BlueLearnerHub adopts similar principles as international best practice and in compliance with India's IT Act, 2000 and associated rules.

## 1. Copyright Protection Framework

### 1.1 Our Commitment

BlueLearnerHub is committed to:

### 1.2 Protected Works

Copyright protection applies to:

## 2. Reporting Copyright Infringement

### 2.1 How to Submit a Notice

If you believe your copyrighted work has been copied in a way that constitutes copyright infringement, please provide our Designated Copyright Agent with a written notice containing:

**Required Information (per DMCA/IT Act):**

```
1. IDENTIFICATION OF THE COPYRIGHTED WORK
   - Title of the copyrighted work
   - Description of the copyrighted work
   - Location/URL where the infringement occurs

2. YOUR CONTACT INFORMATION
   - Full legal name
   - Address (physical or electronic)
   - Phone number
   - Email address

3. YOUR SIGNATURE
   - Physical or electronic signature of the copyright owner
   - Or authorized representative

4. STATEMENT OF GOOD FAITH
   - "I have a good faith belief that the use of the material
     is not authorized by the copyright owner"

5. STATEMENT UNDER PENALTY OF PERJURY
   - "The information in this notification is accurate"
   - "I am the copyright owner or authorized to act on behalf of the owner"

6. DATE AND SIGNATURE
   - Date of submission
   - Typed or electronic signature
```

## 3. Takedown Process

### 3.1 Upon Receiving a Valid Notice

When we receive a valid copyright infringement notice:

```
Step 1: Verify Notice Completeness
├── Check all required elements present
├── Verify signature authority
└── Confirm jurisdiction and applicable law

Step 2: Attempt Contact (if needed)
├── Contact the alleged infringer
├── Provide copy of infringement notice
└── Allow 24-48 hours for response

Step 3: Remove or Disable Access
├── Remove flagged content
├── Disable access to material
├── Preserve notice and related records
└── Document takedown action

Step 4: Notify User
├── Send removal notification to user
├── Explain原因 and appeal process
└── Provide counter-notice instructions
```

### 3.2 Content Identifiers

We will remove or disable access to content that:
- Reproduces copyrighted material without authorization
- Provides unauthorized access to premium content
- Distributes pirated software or materials
- Infringes on any recognized intellectual property right

## 4. Counter-Notice Process

### 4.1 Right to File Counter-Notice

If you believe your content was removed or disabled by mistake or misidentification, you may file a counter-notice.

### 4.2 Counter-Notice Requirements

Your counter-notice must include:

```
1. YOUR PHYSICAL OR ELECTRONIC SIGNATURE

2. IDENTIFICATION OF THE REMOVED CONTENT
   - URL(s) of removed content
   - Description of material removed

3. STATEMENT UNDER PENALTY OF PERJURY
   - "I have a good faith belief that the material was removed
     as a result of mistake or misidentification"

4. YOUR CONTACT INFORMATION
   - Name, address, phone, email
   - Consent to jurisdiction in India

5. CONSENT TO FEDERAL JURISDICTION (if applicable)
   - Agreement to accept service of process
```

### 4.3 Counter-Notice Submission

Send counter-notice to:
- **Email**: dmca@bluelearnerhub.com
- **Subject Line**: "Counter-Notice: [Original Notice Reference]"

### 4.4 After Counter-Notice

Upon receiving your counter-notice:
1. We will forward it to the original complainant
2. The complainant has 14 days to seek judicial relief
3. If no action, we may restore the content
4. We are not obligated to restore content pending resolution

## 5. Repeat Infringer Policy

### 5.1 Account Termination

BlueLearnerHub has a three-strike policy for copyright infringement:

| Strike | Action | Duration |
|--------|--------|----------|
| **Strike 1** | Content removal + Warning | Permanent record |
| **Strike 2** | 7-day account suspension | 1 year probation |
| **Strike 3** | Permanent account termination | No appeal |

### 5.2 Criteria for Termination

An account may be terminated if:
- Multiple valid DMCA notices received
- Pattern of infringement identified
- User fails to respond to notices
- egregious or repeat infringement

### 5.3 Appeal Process

Users may appeal termination within 30 days by:
1. Submitting appeal via help.bluelearnerhub.com/appeal
2. Providing evidence of fair use or authorization
3. Demonstrating compliance with our policies

## 6. User Responsibilities

### 6.1 Content You Create

When posting content on BlueLearnerHub, you warrant that:
- You own the content or have necessary rights
- The content does not infringe third-party copyrights
- You have permission for any third-party content included
- Posting the content does not violate any agreement

### 6.2 Prohibited Content

You may NOT upload content that:
- Infringes on any copyright
- Circumvents copy protection measures
- Contains pirated software or media
- Misappropriates trade secrets
- Violates any intellectual property right

### 6.3 Educational Use Exception

Brief quotations for criticism, review, education, or parody may be permissible under fair use principles, provided:
- Proper attribution is given
- The use is transformative
- Only necessary portions are used
- Commercial effect is minimal or none

## 7. Misrepresentation and Liability

### 7.1 False Claims

**Warning**: Filing false or misleading infringement claims may result in civil and criminal liability.

If you knowingly misrepresent that material is infringing:
- You may be liable for damages (including costs and attorney fees)
- Your account may be terminated
- We may refer to law enforcement if appropriate

### 7.2 Safe Harbor Protection

Under IT Act Section 79 and similar provisions:
- We are not liable for user content if we didn't cause the infringement
- We maintain proper takedown procedures
- We don't have actual knowledge of infringement
- We act expeditiously upon notice
