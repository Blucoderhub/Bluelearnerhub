## Introduction

BlueLearnerHub ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our website, mobile application, and services (collectively, the "Platform").

This policy is designed to comply with:
- Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (India)
- Information Technology Act, 2000 (India)
- General Data Protection Regulation (EU), if applicable
- Other relevant data protection laws

## 5. Data Retention

### 5.1 Retention Schedule

| Data Type | Retention Period | Deletion Process |
|-----------|-----------------|------------------|
| **Account Data** | Until account deletion + 30 days | Permanent deletion |
| **Learning Progress** | 7 years after last activity | Anonymized archive |
| **Payment Records** | 8 years (tax compliance) | Secure archive |
| **Support Tickets** | 3 years after resolution | Secure deletion |
| **Analytics Data** | 2 years | Aggregated/anonymized |
| **Logs** | 1 year | Automatic deletion |
| **Marketing Data** | Until consent withdrawal | Immediate deletion |

### 5.2 Deletion Process

When you delete your account:
1. **Immediate**: Access to services revoked
2. **30 Days**: Profile data anonymized
3. **90 Days**: Content (notes, posts) removed
4. **1 Year**: All personal data deleted
5. **Exceptions**: Legal holds, fraud prevention

## 6. Data Security

### 6.1 Technical Safeguards

| Measure | Implementation |
|---------|----------------|
| **Encryption at Rest** | AES-256 for databases |
| **Encryption in Transit** | TLS 1.3 for all connections |
| **Access Controls** | Role-based, principle of least privilege |
| **Multi-Factor Auth** | Available for all accounts |
| **Security Monitoring** | 24/7 log analysis |
| **Penetration Testing** | Quarterly by third-party |

### 6.2 Organizational Safeguards

- Security training for all employees
- Incident response procedures
- Vendor security assessments
- Privacy by design principles

### 6.3 Breach Response

If a data breach occurs:
1. **Within 24 hours**: Internal assessment
2. **Within 72 hours**: Report to CERT-In (if required)
3. **Within 7 days**: Affected users notified
4. **Ongoing**: Remediation and communication

## 7. Your Rights Under DPDPA

### 7.1 Rights You Have

| Right | Description | How to Exercise |
|-------|-------------|-----------------|
| **Right to Access** | Get a copy of your data | Account Settings or email |
| **Right to Correction** | Fix inaccurate data | Profile page or support |
| **Right to Erasure** | Delete your data | Account deletion |
| **Right to Portability** | Receive your data in portable format | Export tool or email |
| **Right to Withdraw Consent** | Stop processing based on consent | Settings or email |
| **Right to Grievance** | Raise complaints | grievance@bluelearnerhub.com |

### 7.2 How to Exercise Your Rights

**Online:**
1. Go to Account Settings
2. Navigate to Privacy section
3. Select the action you want

**Email:**
- Send request to privacy@bluelearnerhub.com
- Include your registered email
- Verify your identity if needed

**Response Timeline:**
- Acknowledgment: 24 hours
- Resolution: 7-15 business days
