Visual Hierarchy & Scanning Patterns: Users rarely read word-for-word; they scan. F-Pattern: Used for content-heavy pages, where users read the top, then down the left side. Z-Pattern: Common for landing pages, following a top-left to top-right, diagonal-down, and then across motion.
